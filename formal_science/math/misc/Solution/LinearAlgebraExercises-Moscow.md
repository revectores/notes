##### # Exercise 41.

The equation is expanded as

$$
\begin{vmatrix}
a-x  & c+di \\
c-di & b-x  \\
\end{vmatrix}
= 0 \\

\begin{align}
(a-x)(b-x) - (c+di)(c-di) &= 0 \\
x^2 - (a+b)x + \left[ ab-(c^2+d^2) \right] &= 0 \\
\end{align}
$$

The discriminant of the quadratic equation is

$$
(a+b)^2 - 4ab + 4(c^2+d^2) = (a-b)^2 + 4(c^2+d^2) \ge 0
$$

Apparently the roots of equation must be real.
